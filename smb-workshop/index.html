SMB AI Jumpstart Workshop

A half- or full-day, hands-on session that turns your leadership team's AI curiosity into a real adoption plan.

"I spent 18 years in sales helping businesses adopt technology. Now I help them do the same with AI — by showing them how to use it themselves first."

What this is

A half-day or full-day workshop for your leadership team — on-site or virtual — built around the tools you already have. We cover the AI landscape as it matters to SMBs today, run a live demonstration of Microsoft Copilot and Claude using your real business scenarios, and walk out with three immediate, actionable AI use cases specific to your business. No theory-only sessions, no generic slide decks.

Who it's for

SMB leadership teams (10–99 employees, up to 15 attendees) who are ready to move past "we should probably look into AI" and into an actual plan — with a leader in the room who can champion adoption afterward.

What's covered

  • The AI landscape and where it matters most for SMBs today
  • Live demonstration of Microsoft Copilot and Claude using real business use cases
  • 3 immediate, actionable AI use cases tailored to your business
  • A 90-day AI adoption action plan your team can execute immediately

How the engagement works

Workshop Day

Half- or full-day, on-site or virtual — live, hands-on, tailored to what came out of the discovery call.

You receive a custom written roadmap your team can start executing the day after the workshop.
